Support Service Level Agreement (SLA)
System Support and Incident Response

[ES] Este documento define el Acuerdo de Nivel de Servicio (SLA)
[ES] para el soporte del sistema posterior a su entrega.
[ES] Establece responsabilidades, tiempos de respuesta y límites claros.
[ES] Lo que no esté aquí definido, no está cubierto.

================================================================================
1. PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT
================================================================================

This document defines the Service Level Agreement (SLA) governing
post-delivery technical support for the system.

The SLA establishes response times, resolution targets,
support scope, and mutual responsibilities.

[ES] El SLA no es una promesa de “estar disponibles”.
[ES] Es un contrato de tiempos y obligaciones.

================================================================================
2. SLA SCOPE
================================================================================

Support services included:
- Incident response
- Bug fixing (within scope)
- Operational assistance
- Clarifications related to delivered system
- Governance guidance during support window

Support services excluded:
- New feature development
- UX/UI redesign
- Architectural changes
- Third-party integrations not delivered
- Infrastructure management (unless agreed)

[ES] Todo lo excluido requiere contrato aparte.

================================================================================
3. SUPPORT HOURS
================================================================================

Standard support hours:
- Monday to Friday
- 09:00 – 18:00 (local time)
- Excluding public holidays

Emergency support:
- Available only for Critical incidents
- Outside standard hours, if contractually agreed

[ES] Fuera de horario estándar no existe obligación,
[ES] salvo acuerdo explícito.

================================================================================
4. INCIDENT SEVERITY LEVELS
================================================================================

Severity levels are defined as follows:

----------------------------------------
Severity 1 – Critical
----------------------------------------
Definition:
- System unavailable
- Security breach
- Data loss
- Business operations blocked

[ES] El sistema no funciona o está comprometido.

----------------------------------------
Severity 2 – Major
----------------------------------------
Definition:
- Core functionality impaired
- No workaround available
- Significant business impact

[ES] Funciona a medias, con impacto real.

----------------------------------------
Severity 3 – Minor
----------------------------------------
Definition:
- Non-critical functionality affected
- Workaround available
- Limited business impact

[ES] Molesto, pero no bloqueante.

----------------------------------------
Severity 4 – Informational
----------------------------------------
Definition:
- Questions
- Clarifications
- Usage guidance

[ES] No es una falla, es soporte.

================================================================================
5. RESPONSE AND RESOLUTION TARGETS
================================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------------
Severity Level | Response Time | Resolution Target
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Severity 1     | 1 hour        | 4 hours
Severity 2     | 4 hours       | 1 business day
Severity 3     | 1 business day| 3 business days
Severity 4     | 2 business days| As scheduled

[ES] Response ≠ resolución.
[ES] Responder rápido no garantiza solución inmediata.

================================================================================
6. INCIDENT REPORTING PROCESS
================================================================================

Incidents must be reported through:
- Designated support email
- Ticketing system (if provided)

Each report must include:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Impact assessment
- Screenshots or logs (if available)

[ES] Incidentes mal reportados retrasan resolución.

================================================================================
7. SUPPORT PROCESS
================================================================================

Support workflow:
1. Incident reported
2. Severity assigned
3. Acknowledgement sent
4. Investigation initiated
5. Resolution or workaround provided
6. Incident closed and documented

[ES] No se trabaja “por WhatsApp”.
[ES] Todo queda registrado.

================================================================================
8. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

The client agrees to:
- Provide accurate incident information
- Designate authorized contacts
- Avoid unauthorized system changes
- Respect governance boundaries
- Maintain supported environment versions

[ES] Cambios no autorizados anulan el SLA.

================================================================================
9. PROVIDER RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

The provider agrees to:
- Respond within SLA targets
- Communicate incident status
- Apply fixes within scope
- Document resolved incidents

[ES] El proveedor responde,
[ES] pero dentro de lo pactado.

================================================================================
10. GOVERNANCE AND SLA INTERACTION
================================================================================

Rules:
- SLA does not override governance.
- Violations of governance may void SLA coverage.
- Support does not include undoing unauthorized changes.

[ES] Gobernanza rota = SLA en riesgo.

================================================================================
11. EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

The SLA does not cover:
- Issues caused by third-party systems
- Hosting or infrastructure failures (unless agreed)
- Client-side misuse
- Force majeure events

[ES] El SLA no cubre el universo.

================================================================================
12. SLA REVIEW AND ADJUSTMENT
================================================================================

The SLA may be reviewed:
- Annually
- Upon major system changes
- By mutual written agreement

[ES] Cambiar el SLA requiere acuerdo formal.

================================================================================
13. TERMINATION OF SLA
================================================================================

The SLA may be terminated:
- By contract expiration
- By mutual agreement
- For repeated governance violations
- For non-payment

[ES] El SLA no es eterno.
