2. PRICING MODEL
================================================================================

Pricing is based on a fixed recurring fee plus optional variable services.

Pricing components:
- Base Maintenance Fee
- Optional On-Demand Services
- Exceptional Services (out of scope)

================================================================================

- Corrective maintenance

- Documentation updates when required

5. OUT-OF-SCOPE SERVICES
================================================================================

The following are explicitly out of scope and require a separate quotation:

- New feature development
- UX/UI redesign
- Architectural refactoring
- Third-party integrations
- Major version upgrades
- Data migration beyond corrective fixes

[ES] Esto no es mantenimiento. Es un proyecto nuevo.

Adjustment rules:
- Must be communicated in writing
- Require client acceptance
- Do not apply retroactively
