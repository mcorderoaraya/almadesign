Service Penalties Model
Support and Maintenance Penalty Framework

[ES] Este documento define el modelo de penalizaciones aplicable
[ES] al servicio de soporte y mantenimiento del sistema.
[ES] Su objetivo es establecer consecuencias claras y proporcionales
[ES] ante incumplimientos del SLA o del Contrato de Mantenimiento.
[ES] No es punitivo: es correctivo y disuasivo.

================================================================================
1. PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT
================================================================================

This document defines the penalty framework applicable when
the Service Provider fails to meet agreed service levels
or contractual obligations related to system maintenance and support.

Penalties are intended to ensure accountability and service discipline.

[ES] Sin penalizaciones, el SLA es solo una declaración de buenas intenciones.

================================================================================
2. SCOPE OF PENALTIES
================================================================================

Penalties apply to:
- Breach of SLA response times
- Breach of SLA resolution targets
- Failure to follow agreed support process
- Repeated governance violations attributable to the provider

Penalties do NOT apply to:
- Client-caused incidents
- Force majeure events
- Third-party service outages
- Incidents outside the defined SLA scope

[ES] No todo fallo es penalizable.
[ES] Solo lo que está bajo responsabilidad del proveedor.

================================================================================
3. PENALTY PRINCIPLES
================================================================================

Penalties are governed by the following principles:
- Proportionality
- Transparency
- Predictability
- Non-accumulative abuse prevention

Penalties are applied per incident, not per complaint.

[ES] El modelo evita arbitrariedad y castigos desmedidos.

================================================================================
4. SLA BREACH CLASSIFICATION
================================================================================

A breach occurs when:
- Response time exceeds SLA target
- Resolution time exceeds SLA target
- Incident handling deviates from agreed process

Severity of breach aligns with incident severity level
as defined in the SLA.

[ES] La severidad del incidente define la severidad de la penalización.

================================================================================
5. PENALTY MATRIX
================================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------------
Incident Severity | SLA Breach Type | Penalty Applied
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Severity 1 (Critical)
- Response delay     → 10% service fee reduction
- Resolution delay   → 20% service fee reduction

Severity 2 (Major)
- Response delay     → 5% service fee reduction
- Resolution delay   → 10% service fee reduction

Severity 3 (Minor)
- Response delay     → 2% service fee reduction
- Resolution delay   → 5% service fee reduction

Severity 4 (Info)
- No penalty

[ES] Las penalizaciones se aplican sobre la tarifa del período afectado.

================================================================================
6. PENALTY CAPS AND LIMITS
================================================================================

Penalty limits:
- Maximum cumulative penalty per billing period: 30% of service fee
- Penalties do not accumulate beyond the defined cap

[ES] El cap evita que una mala semana destruya la relación contractual.

================================================================================
7. REPEATED BREACHES
================================================================================

If repeated breaches occur:
- Three breaches in one billing period trigger formal review
- Continued breaches may lead to contract termination

[ES] El problema no es fallar una vez.
[ES] El problema es fallar sistemáticamente.

================================================================================
8. GOVERNANCE VIOLATION PENALTIES
================================================================================

If a governance violation is caused by the Service Provider:
- Incident must be documented
- Corrective action is mandatory
- Repeated violations may trigger penalties or contract termination

[ES] Gobernanza rota también es incumplimiento.

================================================================================
9. PENALTY APPLICATION PROCESS
================================================================================

Penalty application steps:
1. Breach is identified and documented
2. Impact and severity are confirmed
3. Penalty is calculated per matrix
4. Client is notified in writing
5. Penalty is applied to next invoice or credited

[ES] Penalizaciones sin proceso generan conflicto.

================================================================================
10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
================================================================================

If a penalty is disputed:
- Parties must attempt resolution in good faith
- Evidence must be reviewed
- Final decision follows contractual dispute resolution clause

[ES] Discutir con datos, no con opiniones.

================================================================================
11. EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS
================================================================================

No penalties apply if:
- Incident is caused by client action
- Incident is caused by unsupported environment changes
- Client failed to follow incident reporting process

[ES] Penalizar sin responsabilidad es injusto.

================================================================================
12. RELATIONSHIP TO OTHER DOCUMENTS
================================================================================

This penalty model is an integral part of:
- Support SLA
- Maintenance Contract

In case of conflict:
- Maintenance Contract prevails
- SLA defines operational metrics

[ES] Este documento no vive solo.

================================================================================
13. REVIEW AND ADJUSTMENT
================================================================================

This model may be reviewed:
- Annually
- Upon major SLA changes
- By mutual written agreement

[ES] Cambiar penalizaciones requiere acuerdo explícito.
